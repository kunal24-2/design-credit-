About Our School

Nurturing minds, inspiring creativity, and building leaders since 1985

Our Mission

The School of Liberal Arts is dedicated to fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creative expression. We believe in educating the whole person - developing not just professional skills, but also ethical reasoning, cultural awareness, and social responsibility.

Our interdisciplinary approach prepares students to navigate complex challenges, communicate effectively across cultures, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of liberal arts education that empowers students to become thoughtful, creative, and engaged global citizens who make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Academic Programs

We offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple disciplines:

English Literature

Study classic and contemporary works, literary criticism, and creative writing.

Philosophy

Explore ethics, logic, metaphysics, and the foundations of human thought.

History

Analyze past civilizations, events, and their impact on the modern world.

Psychology

Understand human behavior, cognition, and mental processes.

Sociology

Examine social structures, institutions, and cultural dynamics.

Political Science

Study governance, international relations, and public policy.

Fine Arts

Develop skills in visual arts, design, and artistic expression.

Mass Communication

Learn journalism, media studies, and digital communication.

Why Choose Liberal Arts?

  • Gain broad knowledge across multiple disciplines
  • Enhance communication and writing abilities
  • Build cultural awareness and global perspective
  • Prepare for diverse career paths and lifelong learning
  • Join a community of curious, creative thinkers
